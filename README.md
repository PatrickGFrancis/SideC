## üöÄ Getting Started

### 1. Create Your Account
Sign up with your email - it's free and takes 30 seconds.

### 2. Get Your Internet Archive Keys
SideC uses [Internet Archive](https://archive.org) for free unlimited storage. Here's how to set it up:

1. **Create a free Internet Archive account**: Go to [archive.org](https://archive.org) and sign up
2. **Get your S3 keys**: Visit [archive.org/account/s3.php](https://archive.org/account/s3.php)
3. **Copy your keys**: You'll see an "Access Key" and "Secret Key"
4. **Add them to SideC**: 
   - Click the ‚öôÔ∏è Settings icon in SideC
   - Paste your keys
   - Click Save

**Don't worry** - your keys stay private and are only used to upload YOUR music to YOUR Internet Archive account.

### 3. Start Uploading Music
1. Click **"Create Album"** on the homepage
2. Add a title, artist, and cover image (optional)
3. Click **"Upload Track"** to add songs
4. Your music appears instantly and streams from anywhere!

## üîí Privacy & Your Music

### Your Music Stays Private
- All uploads are **unlisted by default**
- Only people with your app's link can find your tracks
- Won't show up in Internet Archive search
- Won't be indexed by Google

### How It Works
- Your music files upload to **your** Internet Archive account
- SideC stores only metadata (track names, durations, etc.)
- Files stream directly from IA to your device
- No one can access your music without your links

## üí° Tips & Tricks

## ‚ùì FAQ

**Q: Is this really free forever?**  
A: Yes! SideC uses free services (Internet Archive, Supabase free tier, Vercel hosting) that cost $0/month.

**Q: What's the catch?**  
A: No catch. It's open source. You need your own (free) Internet Archive account for storage.

**Q: Can others see my music?**  
A: No, unless you mark an album as "Public". All uploads are private by default.

**Q: Is there a storage limit?**  
A: Internet Archive doesn't enforce storage limits, so effectively unlimited.

**Q: Can I download my music?**  
A: Not yet, but it's planned! For now, keep your original files backed up.

**Q: What if Internet Archive goes down?**  
A: Rare, but possible. Always keep backups of your original music files.

**Q: Can I use this for podcasts or audiobooks?**  
A: Absolutely! Any audio file works.

**Q: Does this work offline?**  
A: Once a track is loaded, it stays in your browser cache. Full offline support coming soon.
